Our Story

Rooted in Faith,
Serving with Love

For over 20 years, Final Exodus Bride Tabernacle has been a beacon of hope in our community. We believe in the transformative power of God's love and are committed to creating an inclusive space where everyone can grow in faith.

Our Programmes

Sunday Service 9:00 AM

Inspiring services with uplifting music and meaningful messages every Sunday morning.

Bible Study Tuesdays and Thursdays 5:00 PM

Dive deeper into God's Word with weekly study groups for all ages and stages of faith.
