A Word About Our Father in the Lord

A Life Given to
God and Humanity

Chief Apostle Dr. George Chukwuma John is a Christian leader, preacher, and philanthropist, widely recognized as the Founder and General Overseer of the Final Exodus Bride Tabernacle and the Spiritual Director of George Chukwuma John Apostolic Outreach, a non-denominational evangelical ministry of reconciliation and restoration. Committed to transforming life through the power of the gospel of Jesus Christ, with a strong mandate for restoration and spiritual revival.

Chief Apst. Dr. George Chukwuma John has devoted his life to preaching the Gospel with an emphasis on healing, deliverance, faith, holiness, and practical Christianity. Through the grace of God, his ministry has been marked by notable testimonies of divine healing, freedom from oppression, miraculous breakthroughs, impacting individuals and families globally through his charitable and humanitarian service.

Under his leadership, the Final Exodus Bride Tabernacle International has grown into a vibrant, Spirit-filled ministry with a global outreach, reaching people through church branches, crusades, conferences, media broadcasts, ministries and humanitarian initiatives. His teachings are known for their biblical depth & revelation, and relevance to everyday life, drawing multitudes seeking spiritual renewal, hope and deliverance.

Driven by compassion, he founded the Apostle George and Patricia Impact Lives Foundation where he championed social responsibilities and outreach programs including: back-to-school support for students, providing educational materials and assistance to children from underprivileged backgrounds; financial and welfare support for widows and widowers, helping to restore dignity, stability, and hope; and charitable assistance to the less privileged, especially during critical times of need.

He is the founder of the Global Christian Rescue Missionary Chaplain (GCRMC) where he doubles as the International President / Grand Commander General (GCG), through which he preaches the gospel, raises and trains ministers, supports ministries and churches, and actively participates and contributes to society with charitable and community-focused initiatives aimed at supporting vulnerable individuals, widows, the less privileged, students, and the wider society.

He is a member of the Nigerian Legionnaire Commission, the grand patron of Unit 47 Lagos of the Nigerian Legion, and a member of the Nigerian Navy School of Music Officers Mess Ota, Ogun State.

Chief Apst. Dr. George Chukwuma John is respected for his humility, discipline, and servant leadership. He believes strongly that the Gospel must address both spiritual and physical needs, and his ministry reflects a balance of spiritual empowerment and tangible social impact. He continues to mentor pastors, train leaders, and inspire believers to live purposeful lives marked by faith, integrity, and love for humanity.

Chief Apst. Dr. George Chukwuma John is married to Prophetess Mrs. Patricia George. He is a devoted family man and a firm believer in strong family values. His life and ministry remain guided by prayer, obedience to God's Word, and a passion for seeing lives regenerated globally.
