About Us

The Church started as an evangelical and welfare outreach to the unbelievers, Christians and the needy by the Pastor [G.O]. This revelation the Pastor got years before he became a real Christian.

When he believed the message of Malachi 4:5-6 & Revelation 10:7, this revelation became clearer, though he never thought of becoming a minister but to serve and worship God.

Before he started worshipping with Grace & Truth Tabernacle, Ifako (an end time church) God had revealed to him the message of Malachi 4:5-6 & Revelation 10:7.

After many years of worship and service in the above as a believer and later a minister, a revelation about his calling and ministry started coming within and about the above church. The Pastor of the church (Late Pastor John Ogwu) at the first approach when he heard about the revelations gave him his right hand of fellowship.

While in the above church, he was doing evangelical work approved by the church. God used him to preach to people, Churches, ministries & denominations who believed and were baptized in the name of the Lord Jesus Christ and came into the bride. God manifested his power of resurrection through him, and used him to establish churches and ministries which he handed all to the above Pastor and church.

After receiving the right hand of fellowship from his former Pastor (Late Pastor John Ogwu), he launched his ministry around 1998, after some times, the church known and addressed as "The way, the truth, and the life tabernacle", as at then and fully operational from 2nd of May, 1999.

The church/ministry were officially incorporated on 5th of February 2019 & 26th of February 2019 respectively.

While the church was incorporated with the name "Final Exodus Bride Tabernacle", the ministry was incorporated with the name "George Chukwuma John Apostolic Outreach".

The church is an end-time church, which believes the message Malachi 4:5-6, Revelation 10:7. The ministry is a non-denominational ministry or reconciliation & restoration. The ministry gives open hand to all believers to bring them to Christ and establish them in Christ, manifesting the power of resurrection to all unbelievers and believers.

Our Foundation

Mission & Vision

God has called, chosen, ordained, anointed and sent our Pastor to continue the work where the Prophet of our age (William Marrion Branham) stopped. God has sent him to preach to five group of people:

1. The totally lost for a witness against them.
2. The lost sheep of God to bring them to Christ.
3. To preach to children of God who have received Christ but ended in wrong hands e.g. Ministers, ministries, churches, denominations etc., to let them know the truth and to stand in the totality of the word of God.
4. To preach to ministers, ministries, denomination, churches and all believers to let the know God's program at this end time.
5. To preach to believers of Malachi 4:5-6, Revelation 10:7, to let them know that the message of the day has moved into the message of the hour.

God has given him five topic to preach, which types as a building which God's children are (Temple of God. 1cor 3:9-12)

1. Grace: The land on which the house is built. For no one builds a house without land for without grace you can not make it. (zac 4 vs 6, Tit 2 vs 11 - 12)
2. Holiness (Righteousness): Which is good foundation of Christianity. A building without good foundation will finally collapse. (Psalm 29 vs 2, Hebrews 12 vs 14)
3. Doctrine of Jesus Christ: which types partitioning of the building i.e., do's and don't in Christianity. (1 Tim 4 vs 6, Titus 2 vs 10)
4. Mysteries of the kingdom of God: which types the roofing, doors and windows of the building. (Mark 4 vs 9 - 17)
5. Signs and wonders: Which types decoration (beautification of the building). (Mark 16 vs 15 - 18, John 4 vs 48)

Our ministry has to do with five fold ministry as ordained by God:

A. Teaching
B. Pastoring
C. Evangelism (outreaching)
D. Prophetic
E. Apostolic (establishment of Churches & Ministries, Overseeing, Supporting, assisting Churches, Ministering, fellowship and raising & preparing Ministers)

We do our best to stand in the totality of the word of God. We believe in the leadership of the Holy Ghost not man.

The church has it address at number 2/4, Tom Hendrix Okogie Avenue, Off Suberu Oje Road, Casso B/Stop, Alagbado, Lagos.

While the ministry has it permanent address at Wasimi, Abeokuta Express Road, Ogun state, but for now we can be accessed at the church address.


Our mission is simple: Love God, Love People, Make Disciples. Everything we do flows from this calling.

Leadership

Meet Our General Overseer and Father in the Faith

Apostle George .C. John

Apostle George
Chukwuma John

Senior Pastor & Founder — Final Exodus Bride Tabernacle

Apostle George Chukwuma John was born on the 4th of April, 1953 to the family of late Pa John and Madam Felicia Ajoku of Ihiagwa, in Owerri West L.G.A, Imo State. He had his primary education at St. John's Primary School, Ihiagwa, and proceeded to St. Catherine Secondary School Mbieri, Imo State.

He served on the Biafran side during the Civil War (1967–1970). After the war he relocated to Lagos, working briefly at Golden Crown Chinese Restaurant before founding George Hawkins International Company Limited and George Hawkins Intercontinental between 1978 and 1999.

Apostle George holds a Doctorate in Chaplaincy Awareness and a Doctorate in Christian Ethics from Perfection of Christian University of Theology. He served as a Major General in the International School of Chaplaincy and as SSA on Mobilisation/Expansion to the International Vice President/African Commandant and established a chaplain organisation

He currently serves as the International President and Grand Commander General of the Global Rescue Missionary Chaplain, headquartered at 2/4 Tom Hendrix Okogie Avenue, Alagbado, Lagos State, with commands across the world. He is a member of the Nigerian Legion, holding the rank of Captain , and served as Patron and Ambassador to 37AB Vulcan WOs & SGTs Officers Mess, Alamala Barracks, Abeokuta and presently serving as a honorable member of nigerian navy officers mess.

Apostle is happily married to Mrs. Patricia Chinwe George, and is blessed with six children and grandchildren.

What We Believe

Some of Our Beliefs

The Bible

Matt 5 vs 18, Rev 22 vs 18 - 19

We believe the Bible which is the Absolute and Word of God in print, the supreme guide for all matters of faith and practice.

The Prophet of our age William Marrion Branham

God's messanger to the Laodicea church age and fulfilment of 1 Thessalonians 4 vs 16. The interpreter of God's Word for the 7th and last age, the consummation of Malachi 4 vs 5-6, Revelation 10 vs 7

Salvation

John 3 vs 3 - 5, Acts 2 vs 37 - 38, Eph 5 vs 26 - 27

We believe that salvation comes through belief in Jesus Christ, baptism in the Name of our Lord Jesus Christ and sanctification which is the washing by the water of the Word.

The Church

Matt 16 vs 13 - 19

We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, discipleship, ministry, and evangelism.

The Holy Spirit

Eph 4 vs 30

We believe the Holy Spirit indwells every believer, empowering them to live a godly life and fulfil God's purposes.

The Return of Christ

1 Thessalonians 4 vs 16 - 18

We believe in the personal, visible, and imminent return of our Lord Jesus Christ and the resurrection of the dead.
